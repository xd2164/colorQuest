📋 How to play

🗺️The board is an 8×8 grid of colored tiles. You start bottom-left 👤, the AI starts top-right 🤖.
🎨Hover a color to preview which tiles you'd claim. Click to pick. Every touching tile of that color gets absorbed into your territory.
🏰Place up to 3 towers on neutral tiles to block the AI's path. Great for experimenting with constraints.
🏆Claim more than half the board to win — or have the most cells when no more moves are possible.
🧠Your AI Coach explains which Big Idea is in action after each move — in plain language.
